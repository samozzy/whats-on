Julius Caesar: The Musical

'He was a traitor boy, he killed a dictator boy...'. All roads lead to Rome with Bristol Shakespeare Festival's riotously daft take on the original political thriller! Find out if you're Team Brutus or Team Julius to the timeless tunes of The Beatles, the Bee Gees, Mary Poppins, My Chemical Romance and a myriad more – all ridiculously rewritten to tell the story of Rome's most perforated politician. Bring your friends – but watch your back...! Praise for Macbeth: The Musical: 'Laugh out loud" **** (BroadwayWorld.com). 'Psychotically upbeat' **** (TheWeeReview.com).
